## Initial Product Judgment

Scout should not launch as a generic hosted crawler SaaS. Firecrawl,
ScrapingBee, ScraperAPI, Zyte, Browserbase, Apify, Exa, Tavily, Diffbot, and
Jina already cover adjacent categories with stronger single-axis stories.

Scout's best wedge is:

> local-first acquisition for AI workflows, with evidence, browser/user-session
> capture, typed records, and artifact folders.

## Open Decisions For Arijit

1. Whether Scout should be open-source, source-available, or closed-source.
2. Whether hosted Scout is a real product at launch or only a beta/API preview.
3. Whether the $22 one-time hosted price is a hard requirement or a beta-credit
   experiment.
4. Whether browser/user-session capture is launch-critical or roadmap.
5. Whether the launch website should be inside this repo or a separate site repo
   after messaging stabilizes.
