Free local beta. Metered hosted convenience.

The economic rule is simple: local runs use your compute, so local beta can be free. Hosted runs use Scout workers, browsers, storage, and support, so hosted usage must be capped.

Limits

A one-time pass cannot economically support millions of calls. Hosted Scout must meter usage from day one.
