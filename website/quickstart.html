1 · Getting started

Get your key

Sign up with your email. Your API key arrives in your inbox within a minute or two — 5,000 free credits, no card.

Make your first call

Point Scout at any page:

curl -X POST https://scout.chowmes.com/v1/hosted/scrape \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"url": "https://example.com"}'

Read the result

You get clean content plus an evidence block — the source URL, a fingerprint of the page, and whether anything was blocked. That evidence comes back on every call, so you always know where your data came from.

2 · Endpoints

One key works for all of them. Swap the path, keep the shape.

Scrape — one page

POST /v1/hosted/scrape · {"url": "…"} → the page as clean text.

Crawl — a whole site

POST /v1/hosted/crawl · {"url": "…", "max_pages": 25} → every page, one record each.

Map — list a site's URLs

POST /v1/hosted/map · {"url": "…"} → every URL Scout can discover, fast.

Products — catalogs

POST /v1/hosted/products · {"url": "…"} → product records (name, price, link), deduplicated.

Company — one dossier

POST /v1/hosted/run/company · {"url": "…"} → who they are, careers, investors, news — one structured report.

Screenshot — visual proof

POST /v1/hosted/screenshot · {"url": "…"} → a full-page capture, kept as evidence.

4 · Check your usage

Two quick calls tell you where you stand at any time:

GET /v1/hosted/me — your plan and how many credits you have left.

GET /v1/hosted/usage — a receipt of every call you've made and what it cost.

Keep your key private. Treat it like a password: never paste it into frontend code, public repos, or screenshots.

5 · Help

Questions, stuck, or lost your key? Email support@scout.chowmes.com — it reaches the founder directly. Include your account email; lost keys are reissued from support (the old key is disabled).
