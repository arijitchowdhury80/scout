title: Support

---

Questions, stuck, or lost your key? Email **[support@scout.chowmes.com](mailto:support@scout.chowmes.com)** — it reaches the founder directly.

Include your account email in the message. Lost keys are reissued from support: the old key is disabled and a new one is emailed to the same address.

There is no self-serve key reissue in the product UI — this is by design. If you need a replacement key, email support.
